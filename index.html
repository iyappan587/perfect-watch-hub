Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you shop with Perfect Watch Hub.

We collect the details you provide when you place an order or contact us — name, phone number, delivery address, and email. We also collect basic browsing data (pages viewed, device type) to keep the site fast and secure. We never collect payment card details directly; those are handled by our payment or WhatsApp checkout partners.
Your information is used to process orders, arrange delivery, respond to enquiries on WhatsApp or email, and — only if you opt in — to send occasional newsletter updates. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
This site uses minimal, functional cookies to remember your cart, wishlist, and theme preference on your device. No third-party advertising trackers are used.
You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it at any time by messaging us on WhatsApp or emailing our support address in the footer below.
Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@perfectwatchhub.com or via the contact details in our footer. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
